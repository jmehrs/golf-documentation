## Features
- 4.5V to 21V Operating Input Range
- 1.2A Continuous Load Current
- 200μA Low Quiescent Current
- 90mΩ/40mΩ Low RDS(ON) Internal Power MOSFETs
- Integrated Inductor
- Integrated VCC and Bootstrap Capacitors
- Thermal Shutdown
- Output Adjustable from 0.8V
